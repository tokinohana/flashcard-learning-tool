Step-by-step Solution

1. Find the antiderivative of f(x):

F(x) = x³ - x² + 5x + C

2. Apply the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus:

[3³ - 3² + 5(3)] - [1³ - 1² + 5(1)]

3. Simplify the expressions:

(27 - 9 + 15) - (1 - 1 + 5) = 33 - 5 = 28
